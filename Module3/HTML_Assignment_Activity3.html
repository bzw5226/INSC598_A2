Activity 3: Writing for the Web Workshop

Instructions

  • Read one of the articles below and summarize the main ideas/highlights/conclusions in two or three sentences. This this will go to the beginning and serve as an overview of the rewritten article.
  • Read the article again, identify at least two sub-topics, and restructure the article into two or more sections accordingly.
  • Work on a subhead for each section.
  • Read each section and summarize the main ideas for that section in two or three sentences, this will go to the beginning of each section and serve as a “section overview.”
  • Break long paragraphs into shorter ones – one idea, one paragraph; use lists where appropriate; cut out non-essential information and words – keep the rewrite short and concise.
  • Post this on the discussion board before next class.
